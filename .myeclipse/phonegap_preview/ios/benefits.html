1. No Commute. As we know or have experienced. If you work at the typical office, you need to actually be in the office. Or in some cases as in sales, you need to be in the field. Working at home frees up at least 3 hours a day. That’s the average time it takes for a person to dress up and travel to and from the office. That’s three hours you can devote to other things like, sleeping, spending time with your family. FACEBOOK, household chores, or if you’re lucky a part time job.

2. No Extra Expenses (in some things.) You get to save cash, hard earned cash that you worked for. Why? no commute means you don’t pay for fare. No expenses on keeping your office wardrobe updated. No eating out.

3. Less Stress. (some points are more applicable to women) – choosing what to wear for the day -traffic -seeing your co workers -seeing your boss -being late and fined or penalized

4. Eating home cooked food. being able to eat what you want, when you want without being charged an arm and a leg to get it. And hopefully eating healthy foods.

5. Almost safe from external factors. (except of course for natural calamities) -no worries on being kidnaped -raped -robbed -car breaking down -no number coding scheme.

6. Spending quality family time with your Family and friends. .
